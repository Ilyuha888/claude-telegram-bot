# claude-telegram-bot restarts 15+ times per active dev day — source and mitigation unknown

## Signal

On 2026-04-17, claude-telegram-bot.service was stopped/started at least 15 times (07:55, 08:10, 11:02, 11:08, 11:23, 11:42, 11:55, 13:26, 13:34, 13:38, 13:43, 13:45, and more). Most are clean Stopping→Deactivated→Started pairs (operator systemctl restart). One at 07:55 was a real crash (Main process exited code=exited status=1/FAILURE, auto-restarted). Also: 'Failed to write audit log' errors appear multiple times. Each restart creates a session-continuity gap. After the bot-restart auto-resume fix (Action 1), the first-order pain is mitigated, but the restart cadence is high enough to warrant understanding the source before accepting it as normal.

## Problem Type

diagnosis

## Constraints

- Read-only investigation first — no systemd unit or bot changes until a decision is recorded
- Distinguish crash-driven restarts from operator-driven and script-driven
- Do not conflate with the session-orphan fix — this is about restart source, not resume behavior

## Optimization Targets

- Restart source clarity — determine whether 2026-04-17 was a dev burst or ongoing pattern
- Audit log write failure — identify path and permission cause

## Observation Indicators

- Restart count per day over the last 7 days — is 2026-04-17 an outlier or baseline?
- Crash-vs-operator ratio — are restarts driven by failures or manual workflow?

## Acceptance

A short finding report: (i) distribution of restart reasons over last 7 days, (ii) dominant source identified, (iii) recommendation — accept as dev noise, dampen, or fix. Recorded as a haft_note or promoted to a DecisionRecord if it triggers a code/infra change.
