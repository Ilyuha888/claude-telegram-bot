# Verbose pending reminders in Scheduled tab with per-row delete

## Rationale

renderScheduledTab() now shows each one-shot reminder as "• title — today 09:00" with a per-row [🗑 title] button. Uses async reminderTitle() (resolves from payload.reminder_message or original notification lookup) and fireTimeLabel() (formats as "today HH:MM", "tomorrow HH:MM", or "Apr 25 HH:MM" in MSK). handleSchedDel() removes the schedule and re-renders the tab. Alternative: just list counts — rejected, user needs to read what's pending and cancel individual reminders without navigating to the notification.
